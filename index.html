The challenge

Data center power crisis

Grid deployment takes 5 to 7 years relative to 1 to 3 years for constructing a data center. Severe energy shortages have congested transmission lines and pushed $/kWh pricing higher. Edge and remote facilities face high costs, or are denied grid access entirely.

Our modular isotope generators deliver uninterruptible baseload power directly at computing sites. Sell compute to customers today while competitors wait on utility timelines.
